Lasagna

Description

This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. BONUS: it's make-ahead and freezer-friendly!

Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • So on and so forth

Steps

  1. Coat the bottom of the pan with meat and sauce.
  2. Add 4 lasagna noodles on top.
  3. Spread ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, then meat sauce.
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 until you reach the top.
  5. Add Mozzarella cheese on top and put into the oven for 35 minutes at 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
